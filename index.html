Projects

Selected research and software projects in observational cosmology, higher‑order statistics, and simulations.

Marked Power Spectra Constraints from Weak Lensing

Developed and applied marked angular power spectra estimators to Subaru HSC Y1 weak lensing, performing systematic tests and simulation‑based inference for S8 and Ωm. Achieved ~40% tighter constraints over standard power spectra; competitive with other higher‑order statistics.

Preprint

Optimising Marked Power Spectra

Designed and validated new mark functions that can deliver up to 2× tighter cosmological constraints than unmarked power spectra, with robust performance across survey scenarios.

MNRAS

Fast Projected Bispectrum — Filter‑Square Method

Implemented a fast projection technique for bispectra (“filter‑square”), enabling efficient higher‑order analyses for upcoming large‑scale structure datasets.

OJA preprint

HalfDome Radio‑Sky Simulations

Leading the integration of radio galaxy observables into the HalfDome N‑body suite for joint, multi‑wavelength analyses with CMB surveys (DESI, PFS, LSST, Euclid, SPHEREx, Roman, SO, CMB‑S4, LiteBIRD).

FLAMINGO Baryonic Feedback Study

Planning and leadership of an investigation into the sensitivity of higher‑order statistics to baryonic feedback using FLAMINGO simulations.

Low‑z Cosmological Anisotropy with Pantheon+

Model‑independent assessment of quadrupolar Hubble expansion and dipolar deceleration using GR simulations; ~2σ deviation from isotropy in the CMB frame with PV corrections.
